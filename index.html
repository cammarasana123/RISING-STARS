Motivation and goal

We announce the first edition of the CNR IMATI workshop. The mission of CNR IMATI is to provide the knowledge and infrastructure for the development and diffusion of applied mathematics and computer science as tools to address engineering and societal challenges. CNR IMATI aims at excellence in the scientific production, at meaningful presence in high level education initiatives, at participation and leadership in international research projects and at providing innovation tools to the local productive system.

In this event, the CNR IMATI research group will present the scientific activities in the following research areas:

  • Shape and signal modelling
  • Biomedical data processing
  • Urban data analysis
  • Geological modelling
  • Cybersecurity
  • Scientific computing

The target of the event are Bachelor’, Master, and Ph.D students, interested in a formative path at CNR IMATI, researchers from other institutes, interested in scientific collaborations, and industrial entities, interested in collaboration for the valorisation of the research and the technological transfer.
